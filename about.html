About Me

I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology and I’m a third-year student. I’m passionate about technology, web development, and creating innovative digital solutions. My goal is to continue learning, improving my skills, and building projects that make a positive impact in the digital world.

I enjoy transforming ideas into functional and visually appealing websites. I take pride in writing clean, efficient code and designing interfaces that are both beautiful and user-friendly. Whether it’s building small personal projects or collaborating on larger ones, I’m always eager to grow and challenge myself.

Outside of coding, I like exploring new technologies, learning about UI/UX design principles, and staying up to date with the latest trends in the tech industry. I believe that every project is an opportunity to learn something new and refine my craft as a developer.
